Mission & Vision

Creating pathways to work that people can actually step into.

HCE helps people gain skills, support, credentials, and work experience so employment can become a realistic route to independence.

Mission

HCE provides people with disabilities and underserved neighbors the support they need to build a successful path to employment. The goal is not just placement. It is confidence, self-sufficiency, and the dignity of contributing through meaningful work.

Vision

HCE aims to be a leading provider of vocational, rehabilitative, and social enterprise services across New Jersey, New York, and the tri-state region.

How HCE Advances It

HCE runs business services in scanning, printing, shredding, facility management, packaging, fulfillment, and new financial operations while training people on real work.

The Model

Business growth and workforce inclusion move together.

1Partner demand

Organizations buy high-quality services they need every day.

2Work-based learning

Employees gain marketable skills in supported, real-world settings.

3Career mobility

Experience can lead to advancement at HCE or employment in the wider labor market.
