PledgeALLAbilities

Inclusion becomes real when employers change how opportunity is offered.

PledgeALLAbilities helps businesses create workplaces where people with disabilities can be recruited, supported, promoted, and seen.

Employer partnership

Take the pledge. Build the practice.

HCE’s initiative helps employers move from good intentions to concrete action: disability-aware recruitment, better job descriptions, accessible onboarding, staff education, and community visibility for inclusive hiring.
