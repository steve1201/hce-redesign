About HCE

Work changes lives.

It builds confidence, dignity, financial independence, and a sense of belonging.

At Hudson Community Enterprises, we connect people with disabilities and underserved communities to meaningful employment opportunities while helping employers build dedicated, inclusive teams. Through training, support, and business partnerships, we create pathways to long-term success for both employees and the organizations that hire them.
