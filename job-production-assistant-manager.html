Production Assistant Manager

Role Overview

Lead print production with organization, problem-solving, and hands-on support.

The Production Assistant Manager directly supervises and coordinates production and operating workers. The role supports printing and production operations, cost analysis, scheduling, customer interaction, equipment checks, paperwork, and related duties as assigned.

Benefits Listed

Full-time role with a competitive benefits package.

401(k) 401(k) Match Medical Vision Dental Life Insurance FSA

Responsibilities

What this role supports day to day.

  • Make decisions, analyze information, evaluate results, and solve production problems.
  • Gather information from relevant sources to complete print and production work.
  • Communicate clearly with supervisors, coworkers, and direct reports.
  • Interact with retail print shop customers for order placement and pickup.
  • Inspect equipment to identify errors, defects, or other production issues.
  • Perform copying, printing, laminating, delivery, and related print production tasks.
  • Set up and make plates for the printing press as needed.
  • Assist with basic graphic design needs when required.
  • Complete and maintain print requests, paperwork, documents, and job records.
  • Maintain a safe work environment and follow applicable safety standards.

Qualifications

Experience and skills HCE is looking for.

  • Comfortable working in a Windows computing environment.
  • Understanding of Microsoft Office, file types, folder structures, and network addresses.
  • Knowledge of Adobe Creative Suite, including InDesign, Illustrator, Photoshop, and Acrobat.
  • Knowledge of Fiery Command WorkStation.
  • Familiarity with graphic design processes and procedures.
  • Ability to manage workload and prioritize work based on project delivery schedules.
  • Strong attention to detail and comfort following procedures.
  • Ability to regularly lift 50 pounds or more.
